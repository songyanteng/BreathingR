Welcome to the Breath Mindfulness Application!
Thank you for your interest in participating in this study. You will be completing a three-minute breathing exercise and filling out related questionnaires (linked on the next page). Here are some guidelines to help you with the process:


1. You will be asked to select your preferred inhalation, hold, and exhalation durations on the next page

2. Set the length of each duration such that you feel that you are able to fill your lungs with air (not too short), but also ensure that you do not feel significant fatigue (not too long)

3. Fill out the "Before" questionnaire (linked on the next page)

4. Click the "Confirm" button

5. Click the "Start Microphone" button, and allow microphone access

6. Complete the three-minute breathing exercise. Keep the application open until you have completed the "After" questionnaire, after which you may close the application

7. Fill out the "After" questionnaire (linked on the next page)


Hope you enjoy the exercise :)
